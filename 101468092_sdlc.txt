Importance of DevOps in the Software Development Life Cycle

The Software Development Life Cycle is the process used to plan, create, test, and maintain software. 
ususally, development and operations work separately, which causes delays and communication problems.  
DevOps connects both teams to improve collaboration throughout the process. 
This helps make every step of the SDLC faster and more efficient
The DevOps cycle includes:
Plan
Code 
Build
Deploy
Monitor  

Overall, DevOps helps minimize errors, improve quality, and speed up delivery in the SDLC.
