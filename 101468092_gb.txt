George Brown college is a public college located in downtown toronto.
The college opened in 1968 with just a few thousand students,
and is now home to learners from all over the world.
George Brown offers a wide range of programs from business technology,health, design and many more.
It focuses on hands-on learning and prepares students for real world carrers
They also host events to help students connect with employers.
